I am an Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Library, Information and Media Studies at the University of Tsukuba, Japan. My research interests are in human–computer interaction, ageing and accessibility and user interface optimization.

Prior to joining Tsukuba, I worked as a Research Associate in the School of Information at Kochi University of Technology, Japan, where I was a member of Canter for Human-Engaged Computing directed by Prof. Xiangshi Ren. Before that, I obtained PhD degree from from IIT Kharagpur and master and bachelor degrees form West Bengal University of Technology, India.
